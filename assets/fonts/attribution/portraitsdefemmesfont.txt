Portraits de Femmes Regular is a striking display font where each letter is composed of fragmented female faces, creating a bold and somewhat unsettling visual effect.  The fragmented style evokes a sense of mystery and intrigue, suggesting hidden identities or perspectives within the text. The black and white contrast is stark and dramatic.
